## Definition 

> [!tldr] Definition
> A [[Relation|relation]] $R$ on a set $X$ is said to be **transitive** if, for any $a,b,c \in X$, if $(a,b) \in R$ and $(b,c) \in R$, then $(a,c) \in R$.
