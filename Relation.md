## Definition 

> [!tldr] Definition
> Given a nonempty [[Set|set]] $X$, a **binary relation** (or just a **relation**) **on $X$** is a subset of the [[Cartesian product]] $X \times X$. That is, a relation on $X$ is any set of ordered pairs $(x_1,x_2)$ with $x_1, x_2 \in X$.
